About the devolpers


Crop selection

Crop selection is done based on pricing of the crop, market demand, budget required for cultivation of each crop, feasibility of crop for the current land and climate condition and crop productivity compared with other alternatives.


Land Preparation

Land preparation involves study of the effect of any disease from previous cultivation and steps needed to minimize this impact, fertilizers needed, irrigation requirements and costs.


Seed selection

Seed are selected based on price, quantity, average yield, suitability to the land and climate, water requirement, resistance to diseases and easy availability.


Seed sowing

Seeds are sowed at the appropriate time based on weather conditons using the best methods. They need to be sowed at the currect depth


Irrigation

Amount of water that the plants need and frequency of watering needed determines the irrigation requirements


Crop Growth

Farmers need to monitor srouted crops for density of plants, growth rate, leaf size, crop colour, fertilizer needed, plowing, weeding, pest attacks and prevention and harvesting.


Fertilizing

Growth, health and yield of the crops needs to be monitored continuously to check for fertilizing frequency. Bees and butterflies can also fertilize flowers.


Harvesting

Harvesting needs to be done at the proper time and excess needs to be stored correctly. Cost of transportation and market rates should be checked.
